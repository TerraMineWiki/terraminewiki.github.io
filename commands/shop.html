Commands : /shop, /market..

/shop

Opens the Shop interface to buy items that are sold by other players.

Usage

/shop

/sell

Sells a product and place it into Shop.

Usage

/sell <price>

/market

Opens the Market interface to sell/buy pre-defined items.

Usage

/market

/buyer

Opens the Buyer interface to sell at good prices easily farmable items (e.g. wheat, cactus, sugar cane)

Usage

/buyer
