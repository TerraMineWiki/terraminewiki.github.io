Commands : Homes

/!\ : Not to be confused with the /home command.

/homes

The /homes command is only usable if you have one, or several grades. It opens an interface where you can add, or remove a home. You can also teleport to defined homes. To place a home, go to the wanted place, open the /homes interface, click on the + button, enter a name and its done ! To remove a home, open the /homes interface, click on the - button, enter the name of the home that you want to remove. To teleport to a defined home, open the /homes interface, and click on a home.

Usage

/homes

Allowed homes amount :

Grade Value
Gold 3 + 1 (/home one)
Diamond 7 + 1 (/home one)
Emerald 10 + 1 (/home one)
