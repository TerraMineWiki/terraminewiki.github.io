Machines : CobbleBreaker

Global description

The CobbleBreaker is a machine where you can place stone, cobblestone, deepslate and cobbled deepslate.

It will break them and give you loots like diamonds, emeralds, iron, gold, copper, etc.

Side view of the CobbleBreaker

Loot table & chances of ores

This is the list of all available ores in the cobblebreaker :

Frenchylola 2%

Kadaxium 4%
