Plants : Terradium Plant

Description

The Terradium Plant is a seed that transforms into a full flower. This flower may give you some Terradium Beans, that are useful to create Terradium.

The Terradium Plant can be grown on wet/dry soil. It must have a water source/flowing block around, like wheat or carrots.
